Case Study

AI-Assisted Growth Analytics at Wayfair

Developed modern marketing intelligence through customer analytics, KPI dashboards, and AI-generated insights to support strategic stakeholder decisions.

Business Challenge

Wayfair needed a clear, scalable way to interpret customer signals across digital channels and align that intelligence with business strategy. Leadership required data-driven insight that could accelerate growth decisions and clarify the value of AI-powered analytics.

Research Process

Analyzed more than 1,000 customer data points to identify patterns in behavior, acquisition and retention. Built KPI dashboards to visualize performance, monitored trends, and surfaced AI-assisted insights that translated technical analysis into strategic recommendations.

Key Insights

Customer signals reveal hidden value

Deeper analytics exposed high-potential segments and behaviors that were not visible through standard reporting, enabling more precise growth investment.

KPI dashboards improved alignment

Executive dashboards made it easier for stakeholders to track marketing health and connect customer insights directly to revenue and conversion performance.

AI insights accelerated strategy

AI-assisted analysis surfaced actionable hypotheses and simplified decision-making for cross-functional teams, reducing the time from data to direction.

Recommendations

Embed analytics in planning cycles

Use customer insights and KPI trends as core inputs to marketing and merchandising strategy discussions.

Expand AI-assisted insight generation

Leverage AI to identify growth signals faster and create more precise recommendations for customer acquisition and retention.

Maintain executive-ready reporting

Create dashboards that present customer intelligence in business terms to support stakeholder decision-making.

Business Impact

Delivered a marketing intelligence framework that equipped stakeholders with clearer customer insights and better visibility into performance. The dashboards and AI-assisted findings supported more informed discussions and strengthened the connection between data and business strategy.

Lessons Learned

Modern analytics must bridge technical detail and strategic clarity. Turning customer data into dashboard-driven insights and concise recommendations makes it possible for leaders to act with confidence.
