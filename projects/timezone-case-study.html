Case Study

Driving Growth Through Customer Insights

20+ Stores Analyzed
10+ Executive Presentations
Revenue Growth Opportunities Identified

Challenge

Timezone needed to turn store-level performance data into clear, actionable growth recommendations for leadership.

Approach

Mapped customer engagement and sales signals across 20+ locations, then translated insights into concise executive storytelling.

Key Insights

High-engagement locations showed stronger conversion, while inconsistent store performance exposed the biggest growth levers.

Outcome

Delivered a visual, metrics-led narrative that surfaced growth opportunities and aligned executives on next-step actions.
